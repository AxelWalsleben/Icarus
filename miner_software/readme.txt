HOW TO USE:
1,install a TCL environment, as:ActiveTcl 8.5
follow this link: http://www.activestate.com/activetcl/downloads
2,connect Icarus whith one of your USB port, install the USB-COM bridge (PL2303HXD) driver, than you will found a new com port.
driver is at this site: http://www.prolific.com.tw/eng/downloads.asp?ID=31
3,set your info by modify config.tcl, include user/pass and com port number. other stuff can leave as default.
4,click "minner.bat" to start!
